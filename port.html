A bit about portfolio
A portfolio is a collection of assets or works that demonstrate an individual's or an organization's skills, qualifications, investments, or achievements. There are several types, including a financial portfolio (a collection of stocks, bonds, and other investments), a career or artist portfolio (a compilation of work samples, skills, and experiences), and a project portfolio (a strategic collection of an organization's projects)
